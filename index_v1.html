Buy Your Time

Philosophy

In a fast world where everyone is running, we want to pause for a moment and take advantage of the time we've got. We want to seize the day, the hour, the minute, and not only in the present, but for every future moment that will arrive in the future, either near or far away. Dealing with the flow of time has always been one of the biggest struggles of humanity, and materializing a way to buy a slice of it is a step closer to the solution of our big existential problem.

Vision

In a world where the web isn't progressing anymore, we want to build something for the next generations; a new idea that can give back to people the freedom of owning something completely, something that has never been possible to be owned before: time. Through that, we want everyone to be able to express himself the best way possible and leave a message to the big audience watching it, being it in few weeks time o in the next millennium.

Read More

Purpose

In a static social media world where Influencers and Big Corporations are enormously big, and their content is very repetitive and not anymore viewed based on the quality of it, shadowing the value of the voices of small accounts that have just couple of hundred of followers, we created a decentralized channel where everyone could seize his minute and will be able to do whatever he wants with it. Everyone has the possibility to have space and there won't be big differences anymore in terms of audience reaching.

Who We Are

We are a team of five young, friendly and semi-skilled human beings, who want to give people all over the world the possibility of buying what money couldn't buy before: time. In doing so, we aim to lead the world one step closer to a solution of the historical existential question "How do we seize a moment?".
